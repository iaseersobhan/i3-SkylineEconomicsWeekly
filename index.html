About the Author
AIS

Ahmed Iaseer Sobhan

Writer · I3 Skyline Economics Weekly

Ahmed Iaseer Sobhan is the founder and sole author of I3 Skyline Economics Weekly, an independent publication focused on the intersections of economics, law, property and public policy.

Each week, Iaseer explores how institutions, incentives and legal frameworks shape the economic world — from inheritance tax and land ownership to climate governance and market failure. His writing aims to bridge rigorous economic thinking with real-world institutional analysis, drawing on academic literature and empirical evidence.

I3 Skyline Economics is updated weekly and reflects an ongoing commitment to independent, evidence-driven commentary on the forces that govern modern economic life.
