Ahmed Iaseer Sobhan

Ahmed Iaseer Sobhan is the founder of I3 Skyline Economics Weekly, an independent publication focused on the intersections of economics, law, property and public policy.

Each week, Iaseer explores how institutions, incentives and legal frameworks shape the economic world — from inheritance tax and land ownership to climate governance and market failure. His writing draws on primary legal sources, academic literature and empirical evidence.

I3 Skyline Economics reflects an ongoing commitment to independent, evidence-driven commentary on the forces that govern modern economic life.

Siwoo Choi

Contributing Writer · I3 Skyline Economics Weekly

Siwoo Choi is a contributing writer at I3 Skyline Economics Weekly, with a focus on behavioural economics and marketing. He brings an analytical perspective on how cognitive biases, incentive structures and consumer behaviour shape economic outcomes.

His writing examines the intersection of behavioural science and real-world economic and policy questions — exploring why people and institutions consistently deviate from rational models, and what that means for markets and governance.

Ronan Connolly

Data & Econometrics Editor · I3 Skyline Economics Weekly

Ronan Connolly is the Data & Econometrics Editor at I3 Skyline Economics Weekly, bringing rigorous quantitative analysis to the publication's coverage of economics, law and property. Proficient in econometrics, statistical inference and empirical research methods, his work applies regression analysis and statistical modelling to examine how markets, institutions and policy decisions manifest in data.
